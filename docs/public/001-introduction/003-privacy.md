## tl;dr

untils stores the minimum amount of data required to provide the service, and only
shares it with third parties when necessary to provide hosting, email delivery,
anonymous analytics, search, and AI-powered processing.

You can review all the data untils stores by checking its
[source code](https://github.com/alexpls/untils).

## Data stored on untils' systems

To facilitate login, your email address is stored. Your password is hashed and is
not stored in plain text. The date and time of your most recent login is also stored.

To provide monitoring and notification functionality, untils stores the monitors,
URLs, configuration, notification preferences, and related history needed to detect
changes and send notifications.

## Service providers

[Hetzner](https://www.hetzner.com/) provides web hosting services for untils. The
data stored above resides on Hetzner servers in the EU.

[xAI](https://x.ai/) is used as the LLM provider for untils' agents.
Monitor content and configuration are processed by xAI, as well as results.

[Brave Search](https://search.brave.com/) is used to retrieve search results for
monitors.

[Amazon Web Services](https://aws.amazon.com/) is used to send emails from untils.
Email addresses and the contents of emails sent by untils are processed by AWS for
email delivery.

Anonymous analytics for visits to untils.com are captured with
[Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io). IP addresses are not stored by
Plausible, and browser "Do Not Track" settings are respected. For more information,
see [Plausible's data policy](https://plausible.io/data-policy).

No other third party data processors are used.

## Cookies

Only essential cookies are used, such as a session identifier to keep you logged in
to untils. These cookies may be deleted at any time using browser settings. Deleting
them will log you out of untils.
